Style Guide Outline
Color Palette
Background: #FCF9F4 (main), #FFFEFB (sidebar), #ece7df (borders)
Accent: #D96E36 (primary button, focus ring)
Text: #222 (main), #666 (secondary), #D96E36 (accent)
Cards & Containers
Use bg-white or bg-[#FCF9F4]
Border: border border-[#ece7df]
No box-shadow: shadow-none
Rounded corners: rounded or rounded-lg
Generous padding: p-4, p-6
Inputs & Textareas
Border: border border-[#ece7df]
Background: bg-[#FCF9F4]
Rounded: rounded
Focus: focus:outline-none focus:ring-2 focus:ring-[#D96E36]/30
Padding: p-2
Buttons
Primary: bg-[#D96E36] hover:bg-[#D96E36]/90 text-white
Outline: border border-[#ece7df] bg-white text-[#D96E36]
Ghost: bg-transparent text-[#D96E36]
Rounded: rounded
Font: font-semibold or font-medium
Skill Tags & Pills
Background: bg-[#FCF9F4]
Border: border border-[#ece7df]
Rounded: rounded
Padding: px-2 py-1
Font: text-sm font-medium
Typography
Headings: font-bold or font-semibold, tight tracking, generous line height
Body: text-base or text-sm, text-gray-700 or text-gray-500 for secondary
Spacing
Use gap-4, gap-6, or mb-4 for vertical rhythm
Use max-w-md or max-w-2xl for form/card width
Other
Use consistent focus and hover states
Avoid visual clutter; keep UI elements minimal and functional
